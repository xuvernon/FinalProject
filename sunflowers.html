Wondering how to grow sunflowers?

Wisteria black dragon are heavily blooming from spring to early/mid-summer it will lose all its leaves in autumn, then fresh new foliage appears again each spring. 'Black Dragon' is a Wisteria with dark purple, lightly-fragrant flowers. Blooms appear in succession from the vine base.

    steps:

  • step 1 : planting

    • Plant your wisteria in summer or spring. Plant them in a place withe full sun or light and well-drained soil.

  • step 2 : conditons

    • Full sun is best for them. Make sure your soil is not soggy, as overly watered soil will become problematic.

  • step 3 : addtional care

    • To enhance flowering it requires ample space for the roots to 'run' and pruning rigorously twice a year.

    Wisteria needs a lot of care and pruning every flowering season

Tips For Healthier Plants

  • Tip 1. Plant them in fertile, well-drained soil.
  • Tip 2. Prepare soil in an area two to three feet in diameter and 18 to 24 inches deep.
  • Tip 3. Gardeners usually have to prune their Wisteria to keep it from getting too large, but a good pruning can also encourage growth.
  • Tip 4. To help your Wisteria grow taller, give it something vertical to climb up.
